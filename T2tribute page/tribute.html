Tribute to Lata Mangeshkar

One of the greatest playback singers of Indian cinema

Lata Mangeshkar is an Indian playback singer and music director. She is one of the most iconic singers of Indian cinema and has recorded songs in over 36 languages. Lata Mangeshkar started her career in 1942 and since then has won numerous awards, including four National Film Awards and 11 Filmfare Awards.

Some of her most popular songs include "Aye Mere Watan Ke Logo", "Lag Jaa Gale", "Tere Bina Zindagi Se", and "Piya Tose Naina Laage Re". Her melodious voice and impeccable singing style have won her millions of fans around the world.

Lata Mangeshkar is also known for her philanthropic work. She has donated to various charitable causes and has established the Lata Mangeshkar Medical Foundation to support medical research and aid for the poor.
